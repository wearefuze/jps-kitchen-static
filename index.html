Complete Event Catering & Management 


At JP's Kitchen, we specialise in off site catering, providing you with the very best food and drink possible. We offer an array of catering services, including lamb and hog roasts, formal 2 or 3 course sit down dinners, bar hire, paella, and BBQ catering, as well as private chef hire in Essex and London. Whether you're planning a wedding, birthday party, festival, or corporate event, we have everything you need to make your event a great success.

Based in Chelmsford, we also offer complete catering and event management services in Essex, London and the home counties, helping to take the stress away from your event and ensure everything runs smoothly from start to finish. Whether you're looking for a party planner, wedding coordinator, or a DJ or band, we have you covered.
