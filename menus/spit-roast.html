Lamb and Hog Roast Catering in Essex & London


Feed your party or event guests for less with our succulent  Hog roast and Lamb roast catering services, including a head chef and waiting staff during your event. Add dessert for a small surcharge.

To discuss our hog roast catering further, please contact us.

from £16.95 per head
