Weddings

Memorable Wedding Catering in Essex & London 


At JP's Kitchen, our wedding caterers go above and beyond to make your day special. Whether you'd prefer a formal sit-down meal or a casual buffet, or both, we can create a bespoke menu your guests will love.
 
We have a variety of complete wedding catering options available, including a two and three-course table service with champagne, BBQ and hog roast catering for outdoor weddings, and buffet catering to keep you and your guests fuelled throughout the evening. We also offer mobile bar hire, so you have all the beverages you need for your wedding. All our formal wedding catering packages come with crockery and cutlery hire.
 
At JP's Kitchen, we meet with every one of our wedding clients and take the time to understand what our customers want, ensuring the food and drink offering at your wedding is everything you want it to be.
